Why this quiz exists
This quiz is designed for IT support teams who are starting to use AI tools in their daily work. It focuses on practical fundamentals: how to ask better questions, when to use AI, how to avoid bad answers, and how to write clearer messages and ticket summaries.

The goal is not to test how “technical” you are. The goal is to help technicians and managers learn how to use AI as a support tool in a safe, clear, and effective way.
  • Understand when AI is helpful and when it isn’t.
  • Give AI better details so answers are more accurate.
  • Write clearer messages to users and better ticket notes.
  • Avoid sharing sensitive information with AI tools.

How this quiz can be used in an IT organization
This quiz can be used as a simple training module for new hires, existing support teams, or managers who want to understand how AI fits into their support workflows. It can be adapted to internal policies, expanded with organization-specific examples, and built into onboarding or recurring training cycles.
  • Onboarding: introduce new technicians to safe and effective AI usage.
  • Team training: reinforce good habits for prompts, documentation, and user communication.
  • Quality: support higher ticket quality and more consistent responses.
  • Leadership: provide a starting point for building a broader AI Enablement Wiki.
This page is intended as a concept that organizations can adopt, customize, and extend for their own IT support teams.
